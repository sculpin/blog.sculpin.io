Chris Tankersley

Chris Tankersley (@dragonmantank, ctankersley.com) is a self described "jack of all trades". He has worked with a variety of languages including PHP, C#, Python, Javascript, COBOL, and RPG. He is the co-host of Jerks Talk Games, author of "Docker for Developers", and speaker.

Chris is the current lead maintainer of Sculpin (sculpin.io). He helps also maintain the PHP Mentoring website as well as the mtdowling/cron-expression PHP library. Chris has also created a Wordpress to Sculpin convert called Fillet.
